Mozzarella Biscuits

Items needed to make the pasta

  • 1 Flat Pan
  • 1 Rolling Pin
  • 1 Small Bowl
  • 1 Cooking Brush
  • Parchment Paper

Ingredients (8 Servings)

  • 1 Can of Pillsbury Buttermilk Grand Biscuits
  • 4 Tablespoons Unsalted Butter
  • 1 Tablespoon Garlic Powder
  • 8 Mozzarella Cheese Sticks
  • Flour

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350F
  2. Spread flour on the counter and take all the biscuits and roll them out flat
  3. Grab a mozzarella stick and bend it in half
  4. Place the mozzarella stick in the center of the flattened biscuit
  5. Pinch all the sides of the biscuit upwards making a ball
  6. Repeat steps 3 through 5
  7. On the flat pan with parchment paper on it, place the biscuit balls with the pinched side on the bottom
  8. Melt your butter in the small bowl in the microwave and the add the garlic powder
  9. Take the brush and put the mixture on the biscuit balls
  10. Put the pan in the oven for 13 to 16 minutes
  11. Enjoy!
