Blondie Cookies

Perfect recipe for the most simple yet delicious cookies in my recipe book. Surprise your friends and family with amazing flavor hidden by a very mute exterior look

Items needed to make the cookies

  • 1 Large Mixing Bowl
  • 1 Small Frying Pan
  • 1 Small Mixing Bowl

Ingredients (16 Cookies)

  • 3/4 cup unsalted butter
  • 1 cup brown sugar, packed
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 egg + 1 egg yolk, room temp
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
  • 1 3/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon sea salt

Directions

  1. Brown the butter over medium heat, stirring constantly until the butter begins to foam and turns a golden brown
  2. Take butter off the heat and give it time to cool
  3. Combine the cooled butter, brown sugar, and white sugar together in the large mixing bowl. Beat until mixed together
  4. Add in the egg, egg yolk, and vanilla extract to the large bowl. Mix well
  5. In the small mixing bowl, mix the flour, salt, and baking soda
  6. Preheat oven to 350F
  7. Mix half the dry ingredients into the wet until everything comes together then slowly add the rest
  8. Bake for 11 minutes, or until the edges are golden brown and the centers have puffed up but are still gooey
  9. Done! Allow to cool and then enjoy!
