Cacio e Pepe

Amazing simple recipe for my favorite homemade pasta. It is very simple and even someone that is a terrible cook(like myself) can make it with ease and impress the pickiest eaters

Items needed to make the pasta

  • 1 Large Pot
  • 1 Large Bowl
  • 1 Measuring Cup
  • 1 Strainer

Ingredients (4 Servings)

  • 12 ounces spaghetti (Fettucine)
  • 4 tablespoons unsalted butter, at room temperature
  • 1 tablespoon extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2/3 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • Kosher salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste

Directions

  1. Bring large pot of salted water to a boil
  2. Add the spaghetti and cook until soft, 8 to 10 minutes
  3. While the pasta cooks, mash the soft butter with olive oil and parmesan in the large bowl until it becomes a paste
  4. Once pasta is cooked, collect 1/2 cup of the pasta water and then drain the rest of the spaghetti
  5. Add the spaghetti to the bowl with butter mixture
  6. Toss well and add the pasta water in until it makes a thick and creamy sauce that coats the pasta strands
  7. Add salt and pepper to your liking
  8. Enjoy!
